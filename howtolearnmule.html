How to Learn Mulesoft?

Tips for learning MuleSoft - Self Study and Required training

How to Learn Mulesoft through self paced study

1. Open training.mulesoft.com.
2. Click Login and Select Training
3. Click Signup to signup the Training account.
4. Fill all the required details and click the Signup.
5. Once Signup is complete, It will login in to the training platform.
6. Click the Courses link and Click the Anypoint Platform Development: Fundamentals Course
7. Click the Register button. This will help to enroll the course.
8. Once enrolled, we will be able to see the all the 13 chapters of this course which we can go through at anytime.

We can also open the enrolled course from the Dashboard link
